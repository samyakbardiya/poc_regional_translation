PM Modi speaks to Russian president Vladimir Putin, discusses Ukraine

NEW DELHI: Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Wednesday spoke to his Russian counterpart Vladimir Putin, and congratulated him on his re-election as the President of the Russian Federation for a fifth term.

The two leaders agreed to make concerted efforts towards further strengthening the Special & Privileged Strategic Partnership between the two countries in the years to come, said officials.

They also reviewed progress in various issues of bilateral cooperation, and exchanged views on regional and global issues of mutual interest, including the Ukraine conflict.
